Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Shout It! ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Shout It! is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Works entirely offline without transmitting data to external servers

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game Settings: Your preferences for themes, difficulty levels, and game options
  • Game Progress: Number of games played and remaining game credits
  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases (managed securely by Apple)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

In-App Purchases

Shout It! offers optional in-app purchases for additional game credits. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

For information about how Apple handles your data during purchases, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Shout It! does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Shout It! is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from users, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any personal information, your data remains secure on your device. The security of your device data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. Any data stored locally on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
