How do I play Shout It!?

Split into teams, select a topic and difficulty, then take turns describing words to your teammates without saying the word itself. Tap "Got It" when they guess correctly or "Skip" to move on.

Seating: for the best flow, players should sit alternately around the table in the following pattern: player 1 of Team A ‚Üí player 1 of Team B ‚Üí player 1 of Team C ‚Üí player 2 of Team A ‚Üí player 2 of Team B ‚Üí player 2 of Team C ‚Üí ... ‚Üí player x of Team C ‚Üí back to player 1 of Team A.

Use our Seating Arrangement Tool to visualize the round-table seating order for any number of players per team.

How many players can play?

Shout It! supports 2 or more teams, with any number of players per team. The more players, the more fun!

Can I play offline?

Yes! Shout It! works completely offline once downloaded. No internet connection required during gameplay.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the game credits screen and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore any previously purchased game packs.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us at support@picklapp.co with details about your device and the problem you're experiencing.
